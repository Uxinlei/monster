About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an adrenaline-fueled survival game where players face endless waves of mysterious creatures. Strategize your survival with unique character upgrades, powerful weapons, and magical abilities. Experience stunning 3D graphics and smooth controls optimized for both desktop and mobile play.

Game Features

  • Dynamic day-night cycle system
  • 50+ unique monster types
  • Character progression system
  • Multi-platform cloud save

How to Play

  1. Choose your survivor class
  2. Collect resources during daylight
  3. Defend your base at night
  4. Upgrade abilities between waves
